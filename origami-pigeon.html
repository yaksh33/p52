How To Make An Easy Origami Pigeon


Here’s origami pigeon instructions you can fold it easily. You should to use square paper and follow the diagrams step by step，Just need 9 steps you will get your own origami pigeon.

Step 1:

Fold in half to make a crease and fold back.

Step 2:

Fold in half.

Step 3:

Fold the left corner to the right.

Step 4:

Fold the upper layer to the left along the dotted line.

Step 5:

Fold in half.

Step 6:

Fold along the dotted line.Do the same on the back side,too.

Step 7:

Fold to make a crease and fold back.

Step 8:

Make an Inside Reverse Fold along the crease made on the previous step.

Step 9:

Draw eyes and finished.
